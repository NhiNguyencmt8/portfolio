Leona (Nhi) Nguyen

Greetings, I am a MS Robotics student at Georgia Tech, where I do household robot learning research under Prof. Sonia Chernova in the RAIL lab and dexterous manipulation under Prof. Animesh Garg in the PAIR lab.

My research interests lies in curiosity-driven reinforcement learning, interactive robot learning, semantic mapping, and dexterous manipulation. I aim to apply these methods to assistive and household robots that must learn in dynamic, human-centric environments.

Previously, I completed my BS in Robotics Engineering and Computer Science at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), where I worked with Prof. Carlo Pinciroli on swarm robotics and multi-agent communication at Nest lab.
